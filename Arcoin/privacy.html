Privacy Policy for Arcoin

Effective Date: 5/11/2024

1. Introduction

Arcoin is a currency and economy bot designed to provide users with virtual currency management, leaderboard rankings, and other interactive economy-based features within Discord. We prioritize the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting the information we collect and use.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our Bot:

  • User IDs: We store user IDs to track balances, transactions, and other currency-related information.
  • Server IDs: We store server IDs to ensure the Bot functions correctly within the relevant Discord servers.
  • Transaction Data: Information about in-bot currency transactions is stored for accurate balance and leaderboard tracking.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Bot's currency and economy features.
  • To display user balances and leaderboard rankings.
  • To track and record in-bot transactions for accuracy.
  • To improve and optimize the Bot's functionality.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data collected by Arcoin is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized personnel. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect user data, but please note that no system is completely secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any user data to third parties. Data collected by Arcoin is solely used within the Bot's functionality and is not shared outside of Discord or with third-party organizations.

6. User Control

Users may request the deletion of their data by contacting us at our Discord Support Server. Upon request, we will delete all stored data associated with the requesting user's ID.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Users are encouraged to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the data practices of Arcoin, please contact us at our Discord Support Server.
